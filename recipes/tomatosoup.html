Tomato Soup

Description

This recipe is perfect for rainy days or cold afternoons paired with a grilled cheese. It's easy to make and tastes delicious.

I've avoided talking too much so that when you arrive on my site, you can simply know the recipe. Here it is:

Ingredients

  • 4 Cups of Cherry Tomatoes
  • 2 Tbsp of Tomato Paste
  • 3 Cloves of Garlic
  • 1 Cup of Chicken Stock
  • 1/2 Cup of Olive Oil
  • 4 Tbsp of Butter
  • 1 Chopped Onion
  • Spice to taste with Oregano, basil, parmesean, etc.
  1. Heat the butter in a pot, and add the onions once it is melted.
  2. Add Olive Oil and Garlic.
  3. Add Tomatoes, chicken stock, tomato paste, and spices. Reduce heat to simmer and cover for 40 minutes.
  4. Add contents to blender and blend until there are no chunks.
  5. Serve immediately with Grilled Cheese.

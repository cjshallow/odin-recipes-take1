Grilled Cheese

Description

This recipe pairs with my tomato soup recipe, which you can read about here.

I mean, who doesn't love cheese and bread? Besides, gluten free people and lactose intolerant people, of course. Anyways, here it is:

Ingredients

  • 2 Pieces of Cheese
  • 2 Pieces of Bread
  • 1 Tbsp of Spreadable Butter
  1. Add cheese to in between bread.
  2. Add butter to outside of bread.
  3. Place sandwich in toaster oven for 5-10 minutes. Keep an eye on it!
  4. Remove and serve with your tomato soup.
